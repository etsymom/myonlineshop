Our Story

Built by creators.
For creators.

We got tired of watching talented people give 30% of their earnings to platforms that didn't care about them. So we built something different.

2023
🖋️

Founded in Cape Town, South Africa

The platform that
almost didn't exist.

It started in a shared studio in Cape Town's Woodstock neighbourhood. Three friends — a photographer, a filmmaker, and a developer — were frustrated. Leila had built a following of 40,000 people on a major platform, but after fees, payment delays, and algorithm changes, she was bringing home less than R3,000 a month.

Sipho had the same story. Great work. Real audience. Terrible payout. The platforms were extracting value from creators while offering hollow promises of "exposure."

So in late 2023, Naledi — the developer — said: "We know what the ideal platform looks like. Let's just build it." Six months later, InkwellMedia went live with 12 creators and 140 subscribers. Today, we serve over 2,400 creators and 18,000 subscribers across South Africa and beyond.

The mission has never changed: the pen belongs to the writer.

"The pen belongs to the writer. Every creator deserves to own their audience, keep what they earn, and build on their own terms."

— The InkwellMedia Founding Principle, 2023

What We Stand For

Our values aren't
a slide deck. They're code.

💡
Creator-First, Always

Every feature we build starts with one question: does this make life better for a creator? If not, we don't build it. Platform growth is a byproduct of creator success — not the goal.

🔍
Radical Transparency

We publish our fee structure publicly. No hidden charges. No fine print. You know exactly what you keep (85%) and what we take (15%) before you publish a single piece of content.

🤝
Community Over Algorithm

We don't manipulate feeds to extract engagement. Your subscribers see your content because they chose to. We believe real communities are built on trust, not tricks.

⚡
Speed to Payment

A creator shouldn't wait 30 days to access money they already earned. Funds clear to your wallet within 48 hours and can be withdrawn the same day. No minimums. No delays.

🌍
Built for the Global South

Most creator platforms are built for USD earners. We built InkwellMedia with ZAR, African banking, and local payment infrastructure at the core — not as an afterthought.

🛡️
Safety & Privacy

Your subscriber data is yours. We never sell user data to advertisers. All payment information is encrypted and processed through verified, compliant infrastructure.
