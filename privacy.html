1. Data We Collect

InkwellMedia collects information you provide directly to us and data generated through your use of the platform.

  • Account data: Email address, password (hashed), first and last name, profile photo, bio.
  • Financial data: Wallet balance, deposit and payout history, banking details (for payouts).
  • Content data: Albums, images, videos, and descriptions you upload.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, device type, browser type, IP address.
  • Transaction data: Subscription purchases, deposit records, payout requests.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data exclusively to operate and improve the InkwellMedia platform.

  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session securely.
  • To process subscription payments, deposits, and payout requests.
  • To display your content to subscribers and in the Explore feed.
  • To calculate and credit affiliate commissions.
  • To send platform notifications (new subscriber, payout confirmed, etc.).
  • To improve platform features based on aggregate, anonymised usage data.

We do not sell your data to advertisers. We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

3. Data Sharing

We share data only where necessary to operate the platform:

  • Supabase: Our database and authentication provider. Data is stored in encrypted Supabase infrastructure.
  • Payment processors: Financial transaction data is shared with bank or crypto processing partners solely to execute payments.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by South African law or valid legal process.

We do not share your personal data with any third party for marketing or commercial purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Supabase infrastructure with industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit (TLS 1.2+). Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text. Financial transaction data is logged and retained for 7 years as required by South African financial regulations.

While we implement strong security practices, no online system is completely immune to security risks. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to log out of shared devices.

5. Your Rights

Under applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated personal data, subject to retention requirements.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@inkwellmedia.co.za. We respond to all requests within 30 days.

6. Cookies

InkwellMedia uses a minimal set of cookies necessary to operate the platform. We use session cookies (for authentication) and preference cookies (for language/currency settings). We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

You may decline non-essential cookies via the cookie consent banner displayed on your first visit. Declining cookies will not affect core platform functionality.

7. Contact & Complaints

For privacy-related questions or complaints, contact our privacy team at privacy@inkwellmedia.co.za. If you believe we have not adequately addressed your concern, you may lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa.
