Simple by Design

How InkwellMedia
works

For creators and fans, InkwellMedia is the most straightforward creator economy platform you'll ever use.

For Creators

From zero to earning
in under an hour.

1
Apply and get approved

Fill out a short application — your niche, a brief bio, and what type of content you create. We review every application within 24 hours. Once approved, your creator profile is live immediately.

No follower count minimum. No existing platform required. We accept photographers, filmmakers, artists, educators, musicians, and more.
2
Build your profile and upload content

Set your cover banner, avatar, and bio. Then upload your first album — images, videos, or a mix. Choose which items are free previews and which are locked behind your Creator Pass.

First 3 items in every album appear as free previews in the Explore feed automatically — giving your content organic reach from day one.
3
Set your Creator Pass price

Your Creator Pass is the monthly subscription fans pay to unlock all your premium content. You set the price — anything from R50 to R500 per month. You can change it at any time.

Subscribers who joined at an old price keep their rate. New subscribers see your current price. No complicated tiers unless you want them.
4
Share your profile link and grow

Every creator gets a unique profile URL. Share it on Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp — wherever your audience lives. Your Explore feed presence brings in organic discovery on top.

Use your referral link to earn commission on every new creator or subscriber you bring to InkwellMedia. Your network becomes a second income stream.
5
Get paid — 85% is yours

When a fan subscribes, payment goes directly into your InkwellMedia wallet. You keep 85%. Withdraw to your bank via EFT or to a crypto wallet via XRP — within 48 hours, no minimums.

No 30-day payment holds. No minimum withdrawal threshold. Your money is yours the moment it's confirmed.
For Fans & Subscribers

Supporting creators
has never been this easy.

🔍
Discover for free

Browse the Explore feed — a TikTok-style vertical video feed of free preview content from creators across the platform. No account required to watch free previews.

🔑
Subscribe to unlock

Found a creator you love? Subscribe with your Creator Pass for one flat monthly fee. Instant access to every piece of premium content they've ever posted.

📥
Download what you love

Subscribers can download full albums directly to their device. Your downloads are yours even if your subscription lapses — no file hostage policies.
