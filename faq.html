Getting Started
InkwellMedia is a creator content subscription platform. Creators publish photos, videos, and other content behind a monthly Creator Pass. Fans subscribe to unlock premium content. Creators keep 85% of every subscription.
Browsing the Explore feed is completely free. Creating an account is free. Applying as a creator is free. Fans pay for individual Creator Pass subscriptions to unlock premium content.
InkwellMedia was founded in Cape Town, South Africa in 2023. Our primary currency is ZAR but we support multi-currency display for international users.
Click "Sign In" in the navigation. Enter your email and a password — a verification email will be sent. Once verified, you can access your dashboard, browse creators, and subscribe.
For Creators
Submit a creator application via the Apply page. We review applications within 24 hours on weekdays. You'll need a creative niche, a brief bio, and a portfolio link. No follower count minimum required.
Creators keep 85% of all subscription revenue. Our 15% platform fee covers payment processing — there are no additional charges on top. A creator with 100 subscribers at R150/month earns R12,750/month.
Yes. You set your Creator Pass price anywhere from R50 to R500 per month. You can change it at any time. Existing subscribers keep their current rate; new subscribers see the updated price.
Images, videos, and mixed albums. Bulk upload is supported. You control which items are free previews (visible in the Explore feed) and which are premium (subscribers only).
The first 3 media items in every album you upload automatically appear in the platform-wide Explore feed. This gives every creator organic discovery from day one — no paid promotion needed.
Currently, each creator has one Creator Pass price. Multi-tier pricing is on our product roadmap and expected in a future update.
For Fans
Visit a creator's profile page and click "Subscribe." Funds will be deducted from your InkwellMedia wallet. You'll get instant access to all their premium content.
Yes, anytime. You retain access until the end of your current billing period. Cancellations are managed from your profile dashboard.
Yes. Subscribers can download full albums to their device. Downloads are accessible even if your subscription lapses for previously purchased or subscribed content.
Subscriptions are non-refundable once content access has been granted, as content is delivered instantly upon subscription. For genuine technical access issues, contact support within 48 hours.
Payments & Payouts
From your dashboard, click "Add Funds." Choose EFT (South African bank transfer) or Crypto (XRP). Enter the amount, submit the request, and admin will confirm your deposit within 24 hours (EFT) or 1–4 hours (XRP).
When a subscriber pays, funds appear in your InkwellMedia wallet in real-time. Request a payout from your dashboard. Payouts are processed within 48 hours to your South African bank or XRP wallet address.
No. There is no minimum withdrawal threshold. You can withdraw any amount in your wallet at any time.
The platform operates in ZAR. For international users, prices are displayed in your local currency equivalent. Payments are processed in ZAR and XRP.
Go to your Profile page and fill in your banking details in the payout section. These details are stored securely and used when you request a withdrawal.

Technical
Images: JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF. Videos: MP4, MOV, WebM. Maximum individual file size is 500MB for videos and 20MB for images.
Ensure your video is in MP4 format with H.264 encoding. Most smartphones record in a compatible format by default. If issues persist, try re-encoding your video at 720p or 1080p.
